\section*{Closure}

Stage 12 closes the no-distance combinatorial austerity line.

The final read is negative/split, not positive closure: the line supports real combinatorial class survival, mismatch-evolution coupling in restricted settings, a bounded C0-to-DK bridge candidate, and limited structural irreversibility, but it does not recover intrinsic grading, does not generalize protection across families, and does not produce topology locking.

Appendix C0 is therefore frozen as a completed control line. It remains valuable precisely because it ends with a clean boundary rather than a forced rescue.

\section*{Frozen Boundary}

The combinatorial austerity line is now treated as closed. Later work on intrinsic grading, universal protection, or full irreversibility should branch away from Appendix C0 rather than extending the line by reinterpretation.
