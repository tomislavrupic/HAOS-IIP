# Stage 23.5 DK Exploratory Topology Snapshot

Stage 23.5 tested whether the clustered Dirac-Kahler local texture identified in Stage 23.3 and bounded negatively in Stage 23.4 still carries a reproducible local energy-flow topology under small phase, width, and separation variations.

## Frozen result

- total runs: 9
- topology labels: 8 braid-like exchange, 1 unresolved mixed topology
- stable topology families: separation variation, width variation
- secondary labels: 9 transfer dominant

## Why this counts as an exploratory positive

- the clustered DK family did not collapse into diffuse washout under most local variations
- the same braid-like exchange topology reappeared across width and separation perturbations
- phase remained the sensitive axis, with quarter-phase detuning breaking the local topology class
- the result is topological and phenomenological, not a persistence or binding claim

## Clean conclusion

Dirac-Kahler clustered encounters support a reproducible local braid-like exchange topology, even though they still do not establish a robust persistence advantage or bounded composite regime.
