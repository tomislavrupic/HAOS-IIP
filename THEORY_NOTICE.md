THEORY NOTICE - HAOS-IIP

This repository contains numerical feasibility diagnostics exploring whether
structured emergence phenomena can stabilize inside a frozen operator hierarchy
on discrete graph domains.

The program does not claim:

- physical correspondence
- geometric reconstruction
- continuum field validity
- cosmological interpretation
- fundamental ontology

All results are framed strictly as bounded computational observations.

Observed structures - including propagation bands, temporal ordering,
causal influence graphs, or proto-distance surrogates - are treated as
diagnostic features internal to the tested numerical regime.

Any interpretation beyond this scope remains explicitly open and unresolved.

The project's methodological stance is:

reproducibility -> refinement stability -> invariant telemetry -> cautious synthesis

No claim should be inferred beyond what is formally documented in the
phase manifests and synthesis papers.

Readers are encouraged to treat this repository as an experimental program,
not as a completed theoretical framework.
